Welcome to CoachMate

News flash: managing your team just got easier. With CoachMate, you can keep track of teams and swimmers, write workouts, plan your season, and much more. CoachMate is the tool for swim coaches looking to get the most out of each season.

Learn more

All your teams in one place.

Manage all teams and swimmers in one place using one account. Separate teams and swimmers to make organizing and planning simple.

Write now. Practice later.

Easily write sets, set up a practice, and plan your season week by week. You can see what you're focusing on and what needs more attention.

We track data so you don't have to.

Track data for every swimmer and analyze progress; know in seconds what's working and what isn't. Plus, it's all in one place and available everywhere.
