Welcome to CoachMate

News flash: managing your team just got easier. With CoachMate, you can keep track of teams and swimmers, write workouts, plan your season, and much more. CoachMate is the tool for swim coaches looking to get the most out of each season.

Learn more

All your teams in one place.

Here we can talk about managing all teams and swimmers in one place using one account. Each team will get their own dashboard that will make organizing and planning simple.

Write now. Practice later.

We should also say how easy it will be to write sets, set up a practice, and plan your season week by week on our website. It will be clear what the focus is for each week and what needs some more attention.

We track data so you don't have to.

Finally, we can mention how we plan to track data over every swimmer and provide analysis that makes it easy to see progress and what needs to be worked on. Plus it is all in one place so there's no more need for thousands of spreadsheets that take hours to make.
